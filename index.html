02 — Document Packs

Leave your document packs
to us.

We create and compile your Turnover Packages (TOPs) and O&M Manuals through a simple, collaborative 4-step process — reclaiming your time.

  1. 01

    Kick-off meeting

    In person or virtual. Share the job spec and purchase orders, and we set the stage for an efficient process.

  2. 02

    Information gathering

    We contact subcontractors and suppliers and meticulously compile all the necessary documentation.

  3. 03

    Draft review

    We issue the draft pack for your review and fold in any changes — collaborative and smoother every time.

  4. 04

    Final presentation

    Delivered in your branded ring binder, plus a bookmarked electronic copy online or on USB.
